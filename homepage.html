About Us

Here at Cox & Robinson Pharmacy we are an independent family run business with over 250 years of trading history.
Cox & Robinson operate two pharmacies located in Olney and Stony Stratford. We pride ourselves on our friendly, patient centred approach to serving our local communities and we strive to provide our local people with the best health services possible.
Cox & Robinson were acquired from the WR Evans group in 2006 and are now part of the PCT Healthcare Limited group of pharmacies who primarily trade as Peak Pharmacy.
Want to find out more about PCT Healthcare Limited? Check out the Peak Pharmacy website.

Did you know we had an online pharmacy?
Need prescription or over the counter medicines, check out TravelPharm.
TravelPharm are an online pharmacy who specialise in travel medicine so expect to find malaria treatments and travel accessories as well as all your favourite over the counter medicine brands.

Pharmacy Services

NHS Prescriptions

We dispense NHS prescriptions and offer free advice for all our customers. Did you know that you can now get your prescription sent electronically to the pharmacy. Speak to your pharmacist about the NHS Electronic Prescriptions.

Home Delivery

In some circumstances, we can deliver medicines from our pharmacy direct to your door. Our team of friendly delivery drivers operate daily near our pharmacies providing a invaluable local service.

New Medicine Service

We offer tailored advice and a support programme for some new medicines. If you are recently started on a new medicine by your GP please speak to our pharmacist, they will give you the help your require to get the most from your new treatment.


Seasonal Flu Vaccination

We provide flu vaccinations free of charge for those eligible on the NHS. Not ina risk group? Don't worry because we provide flu vaccinations privately for a small charge. Influenza, also known as flu circulates in the UK during our winter, typically November to February. Get vaccinated against seasonal flu between September and November to get the most from your vaccine.

Medicine Use Reviews

We offer comprehensive advice and support for all your medication needs. Are you taking lots of medicines, or perhaps your having difficulties with your medicines? Pop in to see your pharmacist as they can carry out a comprehensive review of the medicines you take and help you get the most from them.

Travel vaccinations

Going abroad on holiday pop in to see our specialist team for comprehensive travel advice, travel vaccinations and anti-malarial drug treatment. Our expert team will firstly assess your trip and then decide on the most appropriate vaccines for you. Rest assured as our team will only provide services that will offer a benefit for you and your trip.

Emergency Contraception

Emergency contraception is available for purchase discretely in our stores. Don't delay, as emergency contraception is more effcetive the sooner it is taken.
